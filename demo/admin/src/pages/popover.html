Popover

JS component for click-triggered rich-content panels. The data-ln-popover attribute is the single source of truth for open/closed state. Click outside or press ESC to close.

1. Basic — Account popover

Click the avatar button to open the account popover. Focus moves into the popover on open. Click outside or press ESC to close.

4. Nested popovers — A stays open when B opens

Open popover A, then click the button inside A to open popover B. Both A and B should be open simultaneously. Press ESC once to close B, again to close A.
