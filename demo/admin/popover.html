    Popover

    JS component for click-triggered rich-content panels. The data-ln-popover attribute is the single source of truth for open/closed state. Click outside or press ESC to close.

    1. Basic — Account popover

    Click the avatar button to open the account popover. Focus moves into the popover on open. Click outside or press ESC to close.

    user@example.com

    2. Position attribute

    Four buttons with explicit preferred placement. The popover flips to the opposite side if the preferred side doesn't fit.

    Preferred: top. Check data-ln-popover-placement in DevTools.

    Preferred: bottom.

    Preferred: left.

    Preferred: right.

    3. Auto-flip near viewport edge

    The button below is pushed to the far right. Its popover prefers right but flips to left because there is no room on the right. Inspect data-ln-popover-placement after opening — it should read left.

    I flipped to the left because there was no space on the right. data-ln-popover-placement = left.

    4. Nested popovers — A stays open when B opens

    Open popover A, then click the button inside A to open popover B. Both A and B should be open simultaneously. Press ESC once to close B, again to close A.

    Popover A — I will stay open when B opens.

    Popover B — A is still open. Press ESC to close me first.

    5. Form inside popover

    Interactive content inside a popover. Tab navigates the form fields; the popover stays open. Submit fires the form action.

    6. aria-expanded live indicator

    The trigger button below has its aria-expanded wired automatically. Watch the status line update as you open and close the popover.

    aria-expanded on trigger = false
