    Density

    Global spacing compression toggle. Click the density button in the top-right header to flip the entire page between comfortable and compact spacing. The toggle sets data-density on <html>.

    Activation

    Three activation paths — see density.md for the full spec.

    <!-- Global -->
<html data-density="compact">

<!-- Scoped -->
<main data-density="compact">

<!-- Default (no attribute) -->
<html>

    Buttons do NOT react (accessibility)

    Buttons keep their full hit-target size in compact mode — WCAG 2.5.5 recommends ≥44px. Density shrinks chrome and content, never controls.
